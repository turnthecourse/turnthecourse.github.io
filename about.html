About

Turn The Course from Schweinfurt delivers the full load of Metalcore. With their latest EP "No One Can Save Us", which was released in April 2018, the five guys show what they can do: fast and technical guitar riffs, driving drums, powerful breakdowns and aggressive vocals. Turn The Course convinces with a modern, mature and brutal sound. The band, founded in September 2014, knows how to unleash its full power at a live concert and to transport the audience with them. They could already prove that on the "Rotten Tour" 2017/2018, which led them throughout Germany. In addition to appearances with bands such as Walking Dead On Broadway there are now also concerts with Imminence or Moments. The quintet works hard on his music, but never loses sight of fun. Turn The Course live their music.
